Usage

This is a guide on how to use this weather application.

1. Search by location name:

This is the simplest way how to search for weather, simply click in the white box and start typing!

2. Search by your current GPS position:

Click on the position icon and just wait for the weather to appear.

3. Submit:

Submit your location by clicking the blue search button to show weather results.
