Connecting Data
and Science

We're a part of the National Center of Competence (NCC) for MATCA (Materials, Advanced Technologies, Coatings and their Applications) at the Institute of Physics of the Czech Academy of Sciences. Our goal is to deepen a symbiotic relationship between Data and Science to further promote scientific discovery. As a dedicated data team within a larger research group, our mission is to make a trustworthy and impactful foundation to address today's technology challenges.
