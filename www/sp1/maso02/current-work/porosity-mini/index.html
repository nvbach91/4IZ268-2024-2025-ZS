Porosity-Mini

Porosity-Mini is a computational tool designed to estimate the porosity of materials from high-resolution microscopic images. By leveraging advanced linear regression techniques, this algorithm provides a robust analysis of pixel intensity distributions across an image. The methodology is specifically tailored to evaluate the microscopic structural properties of materials at the micron scale.

The core of Porosity-Mini lies in its ability to model pixel color variations, which often correlate with material voids or pores. It processes input images to identify patterns indicative of porosity, analyzing these features to generate a precise percentage value. This lightweight yet powerful algorithm is ideal for scenarios where rapid evaluation is required, particularly in industries like material science and quality control, where consistency and precision are paramount. Its streamlined approach also ensures computational efficiency, making it a practical choice for routine analyses.
