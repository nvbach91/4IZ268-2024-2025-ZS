Adora - Redesign of a Goal-Setting Motivation App

Inspiring users to value their time and achieve their goals with purpose-driven design.

Overview

Adora is a redesigned goal-setting and motivation app that helps users understand the value of time. By visualizing how much time theyâ€™ve lived and how much remains, the app encourages effective use of time through Pomodoro-based activity tracking and long-term goal management.

Challenges

The previous app struggled to engage users due to a lack of intuitive design and emotional resonance. The challenge was to create a visually compelling and functionally effective interface to motivate users and encourage consistent goal tracking.

The redesign introduced an inspiring and intuitive interface, leveraging impactful visualizations of time to instill a sense of urgency. Enhanced Pomodoro timers and a streamlined goal-tracking system were added to help users stay focused and make steady progress toward their ambitions.
