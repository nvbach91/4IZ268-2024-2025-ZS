OUR MISSION

We are driven by a singular belief: Borsch is not just a dish but a pivotal part of our existence. It's where we nourish our souls, challenge our palates, embrace tradition, and find warmth in the simplicity of its rich, unadulterated flavors. Our mission? To elevate those precious, comforting moments between the vibrant taste of Borsch and the peace it brings to the table.

THE TEAM

THIS BORSCH WAS FOUNDED BY ANASTASSIYA AND YEKATERINA, INSPIRED BY THEIR PASSION FOR TRADITIONAL, BOLD FLAVORS, AND A COSMIC VISION OF NOURISHMENT. WITH A DEEP APPRECIATION FOR UKRAINIAN CULINARY HERITAGE AND A LOVE FOR INNOVATION, THEY SAW AN OPPORTUNITY TO REDEFINE HOW CLASSIC DISHES ARE ENJOYED. TOGETHER, THEY CREATED BORSCH IN A TUBE, A MODERN TWIST ON A TIME-HONORED RECIPE THAT BLENDS COSMIC INSPIRATION WITH CONVENIENCE, BRINGING THE RICH, HEARTY FLAVORS OF BORSCHT INTO THE HANDS OF TODAY'S ADVENTUROUS EATERS WITHOUT COMPROMISING ON TASTE OR AUTHENTICITY.
