About the Game!

Stardew Valley is an open-ended country-life RPG, which was released on PC on February 26th, 2016. You’ve inherited your grandfather’s old farm plot in Stardew Valley. Armed with hand-me-down tools and a few coins, you set out to begin your new life. Can you learn to live off the land and turn these overgrown fields into a thriving home? It won’t be easy. Ever since Joja Corporation came to town, the old ways of life have all but disappeared. The community center, once the town’s most vibrant hub of activity, now lies in shambles. But the valley seems full of opportunity. With a little dedication, you might just be the one to restore Stardew Valley to greatness!

Features

  • Turn your overgrown field into a lively farm! Raise animals, grow crops, start an orchard, craft useful machines, and more!

  • Improve your skills over time: farming, mining, combat, fishing, and foraging. Level up and unlock new areas and recipes.

  • Become part of the local community with over 30 unique characters. Participate in seasonal festivals and uncover secrets!

  • Explore a vast, mysterious cave, encounter monsters, and find valuable resources.

  • Restore Stardew Valley to its former glory by repairing the community center or working with Joja Corporation.

  • Court and marry a partner, start a family, and build a life on the farm.

  • Enjoy seasonal fishing, craft useful items, and become a local legend by catching all the fish!

  • Donate artifacts and minerals to the local museum.

  • Cook over 100 delicious meals and craft useful items that boost your skills and abilities.

  • Customize your character and home with hundreds of decorative items.

  • Over two hours of original music to enhance your experience.
