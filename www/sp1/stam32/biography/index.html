About Me

I grew up in a charming and close-knit village near Sedlčany, a place that profoundly shaped my personality and values. The simplicity and sense of community in my hometown instilled in me a deep appreciation for the little things in life and a strong work ethic. Growing up surrounded by nature and traditional values gave me a solid foundation to explore the world beyond my village.

After completing five years of elementary school, I transitioned to a gymnasium. My years there were transformative, as I developed a love for learning and excelled across various subjects. I enjoyed the intellectual challenges and opportunities to grow, which solidified my passion for education and personal development. Teachers and peers often recognized me as a curious and diligent student, always eager to delve deeper into any topic.

With a strong academic background, I decided to pursue psychology at a prestigious university in Mannheim. However, during my first semester, I experienced a shift in my perspective. I began questioning whether this path truly aligned with my long-term aspirations. Though I had a genuine interest in understanding human behavior, I realized that my ambitions lay elsewhere. This realization marked a significant turning point in my life, teaching me the value of self-reflection and adaptability.

Eager to explore new avenues, I enrolled at CVUT in Prague. Despite my enthusiasm, this chapter was marked by challenges. The technical focus of the institution proved to be a poor fit for my skills and interests. While I didn’t succeed academically, this experience was far from a failure. It taught me resilience, the importance of finding the right environment, and the courage to pivot when something isn’t working.

Eventually, I found my true calling at VŠE, where I finally felt a sense of belonging and purpose. The supportive environment, engaging curriculum, and dynamic community provided me with the tools and inspiration to thrive. VŠE became more than just a university—it became a place of personal growth and self-discovery. It helped me refine my goals, develop practical skills, and build lifelong connections. The lessons I’ve learned here have been invaluable, shaping me into the person I am today.

Looking back, my journey has been anything but linear, but every step has contributed to my growth. The experiences of success, failure, and rediscovery have taught me to embrace change and remain steadfast in the pursuit of what truly matters.
