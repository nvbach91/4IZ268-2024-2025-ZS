Projects

Elon Musk has been involved in some of the most innovative projects in the world. From electric vehicles to space exploration, his work pushes the boundaries of technology and innovation.

Tesla

Tesla is transforming the automotive industry with electric vehicles, renewable energy solutions, and sustainable practices. It focuses on accelerating the world's transition to sustainable energy through the development of solar panels, battery storage solutions, and the production of electric vehicles such as the Model 3, Model S, and Cybertruck.

SpaceX

SpaceX is revolutionizing space exploration by making it affordable and aiming to make life multi-planetary. With innovative technologies like the Falcon 9 reusable rocket, Starship, and its satellite-based internet service Starlink, SpaceX is at the forefront of making space accessible to humanity.
